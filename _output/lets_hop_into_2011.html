Let's hop into 2011

The end of the year is close. After we unwrap our Christmas gifts, we think about the next year. 2011 has some pretty nifty stuff going on in it; like running out of IPv4 addresses and starting another decade on the Gregorian Calendar.

For me, it's a particularly special year. This is the year I graduate and advance into university. I'm not really scared at all or particularly worried. Finishing high school with a bang is all I'm really focusing on.

Let's put my life aside, however. 2011 is the year of the rabbit. It's a time to take a breather and to make sure you work as efficiently as possible.

Technically it starts in February, but I digress

With that in mind, I've decided to give my site a refresh along with everything else that runs my life. I plan to work more but more importantly, work better.

Unfortunately, I can't describe every new thing is this article. I'll save it for later. What matters is 2011 is a year to take inventory and move forward. Whether it would be my ending high school career or your endeavors as a netizen, let's all hop into 2011.
